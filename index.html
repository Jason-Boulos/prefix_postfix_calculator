Prefix Evaluation

Expression: + 9 * 2 3

  1. Start from the rightmost operand
  2. Evaluate * 2 3 → 6
  3. Add 9 + 6 = 15
  4. Final Result: 15

Postfix Evaluation

Expression: 9 2 3 * +

  1. First operator *: 2 * 3 = 6
  2. Add 9 + 6 = 15
  3. Final Result: 15
